Why get your home rewired?

A modern rewire makes your home safer.

⚡

Less chance of electric shock

Modern installations use RCBOs and cut power in milliseconds.

🔥

Dramatically reduced fire risk

Old rubber-insulated cables crack and degrade over time.

💡

One fault won't plunge you into darkness

A problem on one circuit only trips that circuit. The rest of your home stays on.

📋

Fully up to the latest regulations

Every rewire meets BS 7671:2018 + Amendment 4:2026 — the current IET Wiring Regulations. You'll receive a full EICR certificate, valid for insurers, mortgage lenders, and future buyers.

5 signs your wiring needs attention

1

You have a ceramic fuse board — not a modern consumer unit with switches

2

Your sockets have round pins instead of rectangular

3

Lights flicker or dim when you use appliances

4

You find black rubber-coated cables in your loft or under floors

5

Your home was built before 1970 and has never been rewired

6

You're a landlord with an expired or missing EICR certificate — required by law and essential for insurance
